About $JOGECO

Introducing Jogeco Token, a digital currency that pays homage to the furry companion of Coinbase co-founder Fred Ehrsam.

Jogeco Token draws inspiration from a significant moment in Coinbase's early days, when Fred's loyal companion was featured on one of the very first Instagram posts from the company. As the Coinbase team transitioned into their new offices, and with Fred Ehrsam dedicating the majority of his time to the workspace, there arose a need for someone to look after Jogeco. In response, Jogeco seamlessly integrated into the office environment, earning the endearing nickname "Frisbee Dog" as he became an unofficial mascot and beloved companion to the team.
